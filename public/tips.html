Tips & Guidelines

Profile Setup

Tips on how to set up and maintain a professional Facebook profile:

  • Use a clear profile picture. Don't show up half naked, future employers or team members might see your picture and dislike it
  • Provide accurate information in your profile.
  • Keep your contact information updated and don't share private information like account names or phone numbers which could lead to trouble.
  • Include photos of yourself that are relevant for the people you want to connect with online, prevent nudity and sensitive content.
  • Don't forget this is the internet, everything you post will be used against you, just like in court.

Group Etiquette

Guidelines for participating in coding groups respectfully:

  • Read and follow the group rules.
  • Read the FAQ to prevent asking a question that has been answered many times
  • Use the search function of the group to see if there is already an answer tto your question
  • Be polite and respectful in your interactions.
  • Avoid spamming or self-promotion.
  • Don't promote harmfull or illegal actions. Respect copyright and don't ask for tools to hack other people. If your page or Instagram account is hacked, contact customer support of the page. Programmers are not a hackers collective to steal data back from others

Avoiding Spam

Strategies to avoid posting spammy content:

  • Share relevant and helpful content, your dog or cat may be cute, but that is not content for a coding group.
  • Don't try to sell stuff, there are other places like Facebook Market for that
  • Don't excessively promote your own work, it is nice if you have a blog, but don't put links in every post you see.
  • Avoid using clickbait titles or links.
  • Show that you put some effort in your question or post.
  • Ask questions the smart way
  • Avoid making Seagull posts where everybody needs to respond to get something, if you have free resources just share them with a link or picture.

Content Sharing

Tips on sharing code snippets, links, and resources:

  • Clearly label and explain your code snippets.
  • Use tools like Pastebin or Github (Gist) to share your code and read the group rules and FAQ to know the rules about sharing code
  • Provide context when sharing links or resources. Put some effort in your posts
  • Don't share your API keys, email address or phonenumbers that can be abused
  • Engage in discussions about the shared content.

Handling Disputes

Guidance on resolving conflicts in a civil manner:

  • Avoid personal attacks or insults.
  • Seek mediation if necessary.
  • Focus on the issue, not the person.
  • Don't block moderators if you disagree with them

Reporting Issues

Instructions on reporting abusive behavior to Facebook:

  • Use the reporting feature in the group.
  • Provide specific details about the issue.
  • Keep records of any problematic interactions. Make screenshots in case the content gets deleted so you can show it to moderators
